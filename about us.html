About Us

Founder of Matsyodari Shikshan Santha is Late. Shri. Ankushraoji Tope (Ex.MP)

This is the only engineering college in district which has been started to help the economically weak student to make career in engineering fields at affordable fees. The institute has been started with social vision to develop the rural masses, which were remaining ignored from quality education in technical field. The management and faculty commit themselves to their pursuit of excellence in technical education through dissemination of knowledge, development and technical skills.

The college has excellent infrastructure situated, near to historical city Aurangabad. The college buses and city buses are available from Jalna city to college and travel time is hardly 15 minutes. The Institute has well planned and ventilated infrastructure for each department. All departments have well equipped laboratories with facilities for experiments.All departments have Internet facility with OFC backbone and 32 Mbps leased line Internet link. Seminars, Contests, Short-term courses and various educational programms are conducted regularly for students and faculty. It offers a thorough living and learning experience with its spacious class rooms, air conditioned computer labs, Soft skill Labs. Many reputed companies visits the institute for campus recruitment.
